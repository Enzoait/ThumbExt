3 easy and fast steps to get started

1. Search

Search the video from which you want to get the thumbnail and copy its URL.

2. Paste

Once copied, paste the URL in the searchbar and click on "Search".

3. Download

PC: Right click on the image and press "Save image as".

Mobile/Tab: Press the image for few seconds and press "Save in gallery" or equivalent.
